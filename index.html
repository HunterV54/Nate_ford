Buyers guide

BUYING PROCESS

Why do you want to buy?

Whether you’re a first time home buyer, investor, or just looking to find a new area it’s important to know WHY you want to purchase a new home. Knowing the reason why you want to purchase will help you make important decisions when it comes to finding that perfect home.

Meeting Nate

Let’s get together! There’s a lot to talk about. We’ll discuss your dream home characteristics, the area, and some possible dealbreakers. Out of town? Give me a call! I work with out of state buyers regularly and would be more than happy to assist you.

Getting Pre-approved

Work with a local lender to determine how much house you can afford and learn which types of loans you may qualify for. It’s better to know, rather than wonder.

Finding a Home

I’ll set you up on the MLS for online searches, set up an auto-email system and take you out to your favorite properties.

Submit an Offer

Once we've found the perfect home it's important to put an offer in as soon as possible. I will assist in determining what price we should offer and walk you through the entire process including negotiations.

Contract to Closing

Now that your offer has been accepted it’s time to satisfy any curiosities you have. An inspection and appraisal will be done. This will reveal any undisclosed or unknown defects, this is the time to renegotiate with the seller. Although the lender will carry you through the financial aspect, I will be with you the entire way through.

Closing

Congrats! Closing day has finally come. It’s time to sign documents and make it official.
